About

Our Bears On Smoke are the first generation of our “On Smoke” series which will be themed around the emerging market for NFTs and the Solana NFT space in particular!

Staking

Our holders will stake their BOS NFTs and earn $BOS to spend in our vending machine to get entries into raffles or bid in our auctions!

Utility

We will provide a variety of items to the vending machine from BOS NFTs, NFTs from other projects, merchandise items, prizes from collaborations, physical BOS items such as our hand painted custom shoes and custom canvas, items acquired through partnerships with other projects, companies, brands, and much more!

Our holders will also have access to a variety of rewards from our BOS ranks which include scaling merchandise discounts, access to giveaways and events exclusive to specific ranks, free BOS merchandise, free hand painted canvas of your BOS NFT, and other exclusive offers and opportunities only available to holders and ranked members!
